## Context
What conditions, constraints, user behaviours, or technical realities created the need for this decision? Summarise only the relevant evidence; linked to discovery artefacts rather than repeat them.

## Decision
A short, explicit statement of the choice being made. This should be unambiguous and written as a single sentence. 

## Rationale
Why this option was chosen over alternatives. Include the trade-offs, the reasoning, and the factors that mattered most. If multiple options were considered, list them briefly and explain why they were not selected.

## Consequences
What this decision enables, constrains, or forces. Include both positive and negative consequences. Be explicit about what becomes easier, harder, or impossible.
