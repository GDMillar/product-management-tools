# Playbooks

A collection of **lightweight, high‑judgement playbooks** for recurring product situations. Each playbook captures a repeatable way of thinking - the questions, heuristics, and decision patterns that drive clarity and momentum in ambiguous environments.

Playbooks here are:

- **Practical** - built from real product work, not theory  
- **Teachable** - structured so other PMs can adopt them quickly  
- **Modular** - each playbook is self‑contained with examples and templates  
- **Evolving** - refined as patterns emerge across teams and projects
